About Pushpam Tours and Travels

Pushpam Tours is a tourism company established in Mumbai. Pushpam Tours was established on May 1, 1993. At the beginning we worked continuously on Maharashtra and Konkan-Goa Sectors.

In the beginning we preferred tours such as Mahanarashtra Darshan, Konkan-Goa and Ashtavinayak Yatra. With the support and encouragement of the customers, all these trips were successful. Looking at the increament in customers response and growing efficiency of Pushpam Tours, we took the next step with successfully arranging Kerala-Kanyakumari tour. After that, we organized trips to beautiful sectors like Rajasthan, Himachal, Darjeeling, Rameshwaram, Hyderabad, Andaman, Kashmir, Leh-Ladakh and we are now working on 26 domestic sectors.
