Glimpse of Sikkim - 6 Days

Sikkim is a state in Northeast India. It borders the Tibet Autonomous Region of China in the north and northeast, Bhutan in the east, Nepal in the west and the Indian state of West Bengal in the south. Sikkim is also located close to India’s Siliguri Corridor near Bangladesh. Sikkim is the least populous and second smallest among the Indian states. A part of the Eastern Himalaya, Sikkim is notable for its biodiversity, including alpine and subtropical climates, as well as being a host to Kanchenjunga, the highest peak in India and third highest on Earth. Sikkim’s capital and largest city is Gangtok. Almost 35% of the state is covered by the Khangchendzonga National Park. The Kingdom of Sikkim was founded on the Silk Road by the Namgyal dynasty in the 17th century. It was ruled by a Buddhist priest-king known as the Chogyal. It became a princely state of British India in 1890. After 1947, Sikkim continued its protectorate status with the republic of India. It enjoyed the highest literacy rate and per ca-pita income among Himalayan states. In 1975, the Indian military deposed the Sikkimese monarchy. A referendum in 1975 led to Sikkim joining India as its 22nd state.

Day 1
HOMETOWN-NJP/BAGDOGRA-PELLING

Arrival at NJP or Bagdogra Airport. Freshen Up. After breakfast drive to Pelling. Afternoon arrival at Pelling. Remaining time is free for leisure. Dinner and overnight stay at Pelling.

Day 2
PELLING

After breakfast, go for full day tour of Pelling. Visit Kanchanjunga Waterfalls, Kacheopari Lake. Visit Pemayngatse Monastery and Rabdantse Palace. Dinner and overnight stay at Pelling.

Day 3
PELLING-GANGTOK

After breakfast, proceed to Gangtok via Namchi Chardham Temple. Evening arrival to Gangtok. Dinner and overnight stay at Gangtok.

Day 4
GANGTOK

Full day excursion to Nathula Pass Indo-China border (if permission granted from Indian Army), Baba Mandir and Tsomgo lake. Evening is free to explore city at your own. Dinner and overnight stay at Gangtok.

Day 5
GANGTOK

After breakfast, a full day city tour will be held. Visit Banjhakari Waterfalls, Gangtok ropeway, Ganesh Tok, Hanuman Tok, Flower Show, Handloom and Handicraft Show. Enjoy shopping at MG Road. Dinner and overnight stay at Gangtok.

Day 6
BACK TO HOMETOWN

After breakfast, drive to Bagdogra Airport. Take a flight to hometown. Tour concludes.
